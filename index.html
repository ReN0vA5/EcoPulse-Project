About EcoPulse

EcoPulse started as a student project to reduce single-use plastic in our university. We build DIY bins, run collection shifts, host upcycle workshops, and keep score through weekly challenges. This site is your hub to join, log, and track our impact.

Our Mission
  • Reduce plastic waste on campus
  • Promote reusable habits
  • Create visible eco impact through student action
How it Works
  • DIY bins placed in high-traffic sports areas
  • Scan QR to Join or Log a Recycle
  • Weekly collection & updates ‚Äî leaderboard for winners

DIY Recycling Bins

All bins are student-made from reused buckets, barrels, and paint cans. Each bin is labeled, decorated with the EcoPulse logo, and equipped with a QR sticker for joining or logging recyclables.

  • Materials: old buckets, colored vinyl/paper, laminated labels
  • Collection: scheduled twice weekly by EcoPulse volunteers
