Who We Are

EcoPulse is a student-led environmental initiative from the Faculty of Sports Management at SEU. Our mission is to raise awareness about sustainability and inspire positive action within our community.

We believe that every step counts towards a cleaner, greener, and more responsible future.

Our Key Initiatives

Tree Planting Drives

Encouraging students and locals to plant trees to enhance air quality and biodiversity.

Recycling Awareness

Educating the community on how to properly recycle and reduce plastic waste.

Workshops & Seminars

Hosting interactive sessions on environmental challenges and innovative solutions.
